BIRYANI

INGREDIENT

  1. rice
  2. onion
  3. potato
  4. spices
  5. water

RECIPESTEP

  1. Boil rice with salt & whole spices till 70% cooked. Drain and set aside.
  2. Cover tightly. Cook on low flame for 15-20 mins.
  3. Boil rice with salt & whole spices till 70% cooked.
  4. Marinate chicken with spices and yogurt. Let it rest.
  5. Cook the marinated chicken in oil until almost done.
  6. Layer rice over chicken, add fried onions, mint, coriander.
  7. Cover and cook on low flame for 15-20 minutes (Dum).

For Chicken Marinade: 500g chicken (bone-in) 1 cup yogurt (dahi) 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste 1 tsp red chili powder ½ tsp turmeric (haldi) 1 tsp garam masala Salt to taste Juice of ½ lemon

Nutrient Value (per 100g)
Calories 290 kcal
Protein 15 g
